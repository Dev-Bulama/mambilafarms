🥩 Pillar Two

Premium Beef &
Modern Abattoir

Our abattoir is designed to international food safety standards. Cattle are feedlot-finished for 60–90 days before processing into branded premium cuts — rib-eye, sirloin, tenderloin — sold to premium retail, hotels, and ECOWAS export channels.

Long-term supply contracts protect against local price volatility. Continuous beef revenue stream from Year 3.

Every animal RFID-tagged from birth to processing — complete chain of custody, zero quality compromise.

🐄 Pillar Three

AI Breeding &
Superior Genetics

Our Artificial Insemination program upgrades Adamawa Gudali stock using Friesian and Simmental semen — producing F1 crossbreeds that outperform in both milk volume and body weight.

Surplus heifers and bulls sold at premium prices to smallholder farmers nationwide — a high-margin third revenue stream with growing demand.
