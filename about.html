Our Story

About Mambilla Legacy Farms

A SAB Foundation initiative building Nigeria's most integrated cattle enterprise from the cool highlands of Mambilla.

Vision & Mission

Transforming Nigeria's
Livestock Sector

Mambilla Legacy Farms is a pioneering agribusiness SPV promoted by SAB Foundation with a bold mandate: build a one million head cattle herd within five years on the Mambilla Plateau — one of West Africa's most fertile cattle-rearing zones.

We combine indigenous Adamawa Gudali stock with AI breeding using Friesian and Simmental genetics — producing superior dual-purpose cattle with world-class milk yields and premium beef grades.

"1 Million Cattle. 50,000+ Jobs. ₦4–5 Trillion GDP Contribution to Nigeria."
— SAB Foundation

5-Year Roadmap

Our Journey to Scale

A phased rollout designed to reach profitability by Year 3, full settlement by Year 5, with reinvestment into Phase 2 across Northern Nigeria.

Join the Journey →
Year 1
Foundation & Acquisition

Land preparation, Adamawa Gudali stock acquisition, vet clinics, water infrastructure, and fodder banks.

Year 2
Infrastructure Build-Out

AI breeding launched. Abattoir constructed. Dairy processing commissioned. Herd scales rapidly.

Year 3
Production & First Payouts

Break-even achieved. Milk sales begin — quarterly distributions activated for all investors.

Year 5
Full Settlement

Full principal and profit distribution. Transfer options from Year 3. Reinvestment into Phase 2 available.
